What we do best?

You want to build a renewable energy power plant (Wind, PV, Biogas) and want to earn money with it. The grid connection is a necessary evil that is needed at the power plant. Customers often have little or no electrical expertise.

Procedure

We don't let the little things stop us, we implement them quickly. This is how we ensure that we can deliver fast and powerful results for our clients.

01

First to the basics

In the first step, we clarify the basics for the further project with you. We clarify basic questions such as the project location. This enables us to clarify which network operator is the right contact person for the network connection application.

02

Where are you travelling to?

We prepare the grid connection application and accompany you in the discussions with the grid operator and the energy supplier. We advise you on the most economical and technically most sensible network connection.

03

We develop your plan for you

Determination of the electrical losses of the wind farm, determination of the cable cross-sections, creation of cable location plans and circuit diagrams and coordination with suppliers.

04

We accompany you in the implementation of your plans

Creation of bill of quantities (LV) for transfer station including award negotiations and Technical construction support.
